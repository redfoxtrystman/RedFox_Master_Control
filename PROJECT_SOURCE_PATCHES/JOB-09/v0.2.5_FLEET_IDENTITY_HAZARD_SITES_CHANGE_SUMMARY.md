## Fleet identity functions

Fleet helper functions are grouped under one `fleetTools` table to remain under Lua's main-chunk local-variable limit.

- capture current player model/configuration;
- capture three paint palettes and metallic/roughness/clear-coat data;
- register, rename, select, role-cycle, update, and remove Fleet Book records;
- public read/register/update APIs:
  - `M.registerCurrentFleetVehicle()`
  - `M.updateFleetUnitFromCurrentVehicle(unitId)`
  - `M.getFleet()`

No actual vehicle is deleted when a Fleet Book record is removed.

## Map semantic functions

- Analyze navigation-node degree, neighbor directions, segment length, road width, and grade.
- Produce candidate types:
  - `road_segment`
  - `intersection`
  - `sharp_curve`
- Add `chooseRoadSceneSpot(..., preferredType)`.
- Log `[RedFox][TOW][SITE_SCAN]` with per-map counts.
- Export `M.getHazardSiteScan()`.

## New accident plans

- `intersection_tbone`
  - uses primary and crossing road directions;
  - positions two vehicles on crossing approaches;
  - applies directional impact vectors.
- `sharp_curve_rollover`
  - places a rolled vehicle outside the curve/road edge;
  - places a secondary impacted vehicle in or near the travel lane;
  - applies directional impact vectors.

Existing v0.2.3 mixed plans remain available.
